## CAPSTONE PROJECT (YOU AS SOLUTION ARCHITECT)

- There are no rules. You are free to choose any problem, and you are free to choose any functional and non-functional requirements.
- You will create a PDF document with all the details about your design from functional requirement, non-functional requirements and then as you have learn in the class, you will design the solution for the selected problem.
- Feel free to take any realistic assumptions and trade-offs but ensure you satisfy your functional and non-functional requirements.
- Use any tool to draw the diagrams, and ensure they are top-notch. Consider that you are working as an architect as some company.
- If you have done some mathematical calculations, you just have to show what values you have come up with. This has to align with the system, you will propose.
- Feel free to use any technique that we studied during the workshop.
- You are allowed to use ChatGPT, or any other AI tool. However, you should understand everything about what you will propose.
- Maximum 2 people in the group are allowed.
